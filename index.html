FAQs

Does every tenant have to agree to using DepositBee?

Yes. Under new regulations, we have to get consent from every tenant to dispute the amount being withheld and for DepositBee to act as a third party on behalf of the tenants.

When will we get our money?

We will send your deposit straight to your bank account on the day we receive it from your landlord / agent. This may take 3-5 working days to clear into your account.

What is the time limit for using DepositBee?

We can only raise disputes up to 3 months after the end of your tenancy. You shouldn't be waiting around for nearly that long so come talk to us and we can help you out!

Do we need evidence that we're being unfairly treated?

No. The onus is on your landlord / agent to prove they can withhold any of the deposit. Obviously any evidence you can give us would be useful, but not essential. Remember, your deposit is exactly that: yours!
